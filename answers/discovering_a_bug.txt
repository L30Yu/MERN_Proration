Prompt
~~~~~~

Suppose we discover a bug with our algorithm and investors for two deals had incorrect
allocations. This means that some of the investors ended up investing more than they were allowed to
while others invested less than they were allowed to. One of deals happened two years ago and the
other one happened two weeks ago. Please describe, in detail, how would you go about correcting this
issue and how would you communicate this to the affected customers.

Response
~~~~~~~~
1. Identify which investors were affected, how much they were over or under-allocated, and which deals 
were impacted. Gather all the data and informations. 

2. By discussing with all the stakeholders to correct the allocations and ensure that investors are allocated 
the correct amounts going forward. And, in order to continuesly gaining trust from affected investors, we 
need be transparency.

3. Find the root cause and create Tech Spec document to make sure we have the best solution before we implement
and update any other potential affected data or investment. 

4. Create Postmortem with the details of why this incident would be happen, how we find it and whats the 
timeline with details. What actions involved and how were solved it, where we can improve. Whats the following actions
